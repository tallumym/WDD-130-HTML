-What is this sites purpose?-

The purpose of this site is to supply people with information about the Beatles. This is the site to find all your Beatle question's answers. With this site, you don't need to individually type in questions, all the answers you will find are here.

-What to expect from this site-

From this site you should expect organization and clear answers. You will also be met with an aesthetically pleasing view.
